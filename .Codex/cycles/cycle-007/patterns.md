### PAT-031: Psalm 51 Transformation Chain
**Scripture:** Psalm 51:1-12 — "Create in me a clean heart, O God, and renew a right spirit within me"
**Pattern Type:** RESTORATION + CREATION
**Level:** 2 | **Score:** 8.0
**Pattern Description:** Psalm 51 is a 5-stage transformation pipeline: (1) Confession/intake — "I know my transgressions" (raw input acknowledged), (2) Cleansing — "wash me" (noise removal), (3) Analysis — "against you only have I sinned" (root cause identification), (4) Creation — "create in me a clean heart" (new output generation), (5) Restoration — "restore to me the joy" (formatted, complete output). Each stage must complete before the next begins. Skipping stages produces incomplete transformation.
**Modern Mapping:** Multi-stage prompt chains for document generation. The 5-stage pattern maps directly to: intake, preprocessing, analysis, generation, post-processing. Psalm 51 is the architecture of every effective AI document pipeline.
**Infrastructure Status:** EXISTS NOW
**Application Potential:** Validated as the architectural blueprint for GrantPilot's 5-prompt chain. Each prompt corresponds to a Psalm 51 stage.
**Discovered By:** Chief Theologian + Chief Builder
**Cycle Discovered:** 007
**Build Status:** APPLIED TO BUILD-004 (GrantPilot prompt chain architecture)

### PAT-032: Loaves-to-Thousands Efficiency
**Scripture:** John 6:1-13 — Five loaves, two fish fed 5,000 with 12 baskets remaining
**Pattern Type:** MULTIPLICATION
**Level:** 2 | **Score:** 7.8
**Pattern Description:** The multiplication ratio is approximately 1:1000 (5 loaves → 5,000 people). More remarkably, the output EXCEEDS demand — 12 baskets of surplus remain. This is not just efficiency; it is superabundant efficiency. The transformation produces more than was requested.
**Modern Mapping:** AI document generation from minimal input. A 15-minute form (5 loaves) produces a 20-page professional proposal (5,000 fed). The surplus (12 baskets) maps to: the same intake data can generate MULTIPLE proposals to different funders without additional input. One intake, many outputs.
**Infrastructure Status:** EXISTS NOW
**Application Potential:** GrantPilot feature: "Apply to Multiple Funders" — one intake form generates proposals tailored to 3-5 different funders simultaneously. The loaves multiply across funders.
**Discovered By:** Chief Innovator
**Cycle Discovered:** 007
**Build Status:** FUTURE FEATURE for BUILD-004 (multi-funder generation)
