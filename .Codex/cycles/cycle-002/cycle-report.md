## SCRIPTURE ANALYSIS SUMMARY

### Psalm 2: The Governance Architecture
Psalm 2 revealed two buildable patterns:
1. **PAT-013 (Decree Protocol):** Authority is declared, not earned. Maps to governance tooling for cooperatives and investment clubs that need formal, auditable authority structures. BUILD-003: DecreeDAO.
2. **PAT-014 (Ask-Receive Distribution):** Value exists; the bottleneck is the request mechanism. Maps to AI-powered grant writing — the funding exists, African organizations fail at the application. BUILD-004: GrantPilot.

### John 1:19-51: The Trust Network
John 1:19-51 revealed three buildable patterns:
1. **PAT-015 (Referral Chain):** Trust propagates through weighted referral networks. Maps to deal-flow verification for diaspora investors. BUILD-005: TrustChain.
2. **PAT-016 ("Come and See" Protocol):** Conversion happens through direct experience, not explanation. Maps to instant product demo generation. BUILD-006: DemoFirst.
3. **PAT-017 (Pre-Knowledge Trust Collapse):** Demonstrated contextual knowledge eliminates trust-building time. Maps to AI pre-meeting intelligence. BUILD-007: KnowFirst.

### Cross-Passage Synthesis: The Go-To-Market System
The five patterns form a complete system:
- Establish authority (DecreeDAO — formal governance)
- Fund the operation (GrantPilot — unlock existing capital)
- Build trust networks (TrustChain — verified referrals)
- Convert prospects (DemoFirst — "come and see")
- Accelerate relationships (KnowFirst — pre-knowledge)

This is not accidental. Psalm 2 provides the authority layer. John 1 provides the trust and conversion layers. Together they describe a complete business launch system.
