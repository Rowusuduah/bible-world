## READING PLAN PRINCIPLES

1. Sequential reading — do not jump ahead except for directed cross-reference
2. Old Testament, New Testament, and Psalms/Wisdom literature read in parallel tracks
3. Each cycle reads at minimum one passage from each active track
4. Patterns discovered trigger deeper re-reading of the pattern's surrounding context
5. No passage is marked "exhausted" — all scripture is revisitable for new pattern discovery at deeper levels
